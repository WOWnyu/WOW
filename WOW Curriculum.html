WOW Curriculum

Pre-Kindergarten

The WOW Pre-K program connects to the New York State Next Generation Language Arts Learning Standards.
The New York State Next Generation English Language Arts Learning Standards the Pre-Kindergarten units connect to are:
Reading: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 & 9
Writing: 1, 4, 6, & 7
Speaking and Listening: 1a, 1b, 1c, 2, & 6
Language Standards: 4, 5a, 5b, 5c, & 6
