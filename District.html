District Partnership



New York City Public Schools District 7

District 7 is located in the Bronx and led by superintendent Elisa Alvarez. We partner with 12 schools in the district working across grades in 65 classrooms. Our program supports students in pre-kindergarten, kindergarten, first grade, and second grade. We are continually impressed with the teaching and leadership we see in District 7.



157X School-Wide Pilot

With the collaboration and support of the administrators at 157X elementary school, WOW is being implemented in all pre-kindergarten, kindergarten, first grade, and second grade classrooms at the school.



Teacher Leaders

This year we have six teacher leaders supporting the WOW program at five of our schools. Teacher leaders have strong WOW instruction occurring in their classrooms and are supports for new teachers using the program.
